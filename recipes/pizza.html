Homemade Pizza Recipe

>

Ingredients:

Homemade Pizza Dough:

    3 1/2 cups bread flour 1 teaspoon sugar 1 pack instant dry yeast 2 teaspoons kosher salt 1 1/2 cups water, (110 degrees F) 2 tablespoons olive oil

Pizza Toppings

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozz Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  1. Turn on oven
  2. Roll out dough
  3. Spread out pizza sauce
  4. Spread out cheese
  5. Bake for 35min
